Manchester United

Manchester United Football Club (MUFC) is one of the most famous and successful football clubs in the world, based in Manchester, England. Founded in 1878 as Newton Heath LYR F.C., the club changed its name to Manchester United in 1902.

MUFC has achieved a lot, including more than 20 Premier League titles, 12 FA Cups, and 3 UEFA Champions League trophies. The club is also known by the nickname ‘The Red Devils’ and has a large fan base around the world home stadium, Old Trafford, is known as the ‘Theatre of Dreams’ and has a capacity of over 75,000 spectators. .

Manchester United is not only famous for its success on the pitch, but also for its traditional values, including fighting spirit and club pride. Legendary coach Sir Alex Ferguson, who served from 1986 to 2013, has been one of the most influential figures in the club's history. United continues to be a force in world football, both on and off the pitch.
